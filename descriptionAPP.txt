        
        |================|
        |    Overview    |
        |================|

This is a full-stack web application for a shopping cart, designed to provide users with a seamless and enjoyable online shopping experience.
The application allows users to browse through a variety of products, add them to the cart, and proceed to checkout.


        |================|  
        |    Features    |
        |================|

1. Product Catalog: Explore a diverse range of products with detailed descriptions and images.

2. Shopping Cart: Add and remove items from the cart with real-time updates.

3. Checkout Process: Efficient checkout process with address filling and will be sent directly via WhatsApp.

4. Responsive Design: A mobile-friendly interface ensures a consistent experience across devices.
